== Description ==

Let you and your visitors rate your comments, using simple AJAX and jQuery to update the comments' ratings dynamically. Visitors can either like or dislike the comments, which should provide you with simple, yet constructive, feedback of the content included in them. Also try the [Visitor Like/Dislike Post Rating](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/visitor-likedislike-post-rating/ "Visitor Like/Dislike Post Rating") plugin.

== Installation ==

1. Simply upload the files in "visitor-likedislike-comment-rating" to your blog's plugin folder, activate it and you're done.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can my visitors do more than like/dislike? =

No. That's the point. Visitors can either like or dislike the comments. No compromising or "Maybe I think some of it is good, I just don't agree" stuff. Like or dislike, that's it.

= May anyone rate a comment more than once? =

No... What's the point of that?
